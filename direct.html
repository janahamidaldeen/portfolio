DiRect – Technovation Grand Prize Winner

DiRect is a mobile app that supports individuals with dietary restrictions by offering curated restaurant listings, grocery guides, and personalized recipes. The intuitive user interface was designed with accessibility and usability in mind.

This project won the Best User Design and the Grand Prize at the Technovation competition. As a result, we were invited for a Google interview and featured on the University of Waterloo Faculty of Mathematics YouTube channel.

The journey involved market research, wireframing, iterative development, and pitching to a global panel. Working on DiRect helped strengthen our skills in UX design, agile teamwork, and impactful storytelling.
